```
WHAT DOES NOT RESTORE CREATIVITY         WHAT DOES
─────────────────────────────────────    ──────────────────────────────────
Learning more in your field              Entering unfamiliar topology
Reading more expert opinion              Exposing yourself to genuine surprise
Attending more conferences               Being wrong in a new domain
Getting more credentials                 Acting before you have a map
Optimising your existing process         Choosing the path with no map
```
