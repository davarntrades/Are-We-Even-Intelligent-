## The Question

Look at the lineage of boundary crossers.

```
Copernicus   — threatened the Church
Galileo      — tried by the Inquisition
Darwin       — attacked by the clergy and the academy
Einstein     — dismissed early, worked as a patent clerk
Turing       — prosecuted by the state he saved
Morrison     — failed the exams. Passed art and ICT.
```

Not one produced by an institution operating normally.

Every single breakthrough came from someone who either hadn’t fully entered the deep institutional basin yet — or had been forced out of it.

This is not a coincidence. It is what the geometry predicts. And the geometry has been predicting it for 500 years.

  None were produced by the institution operating normally.
  All were near the edge, outside, or expelled.
  All crossed a separatrix the institution couldn't.
  All were recognised late — by the institution —
  after the crossing had already happened.

  It validates it.
  After the outsider does the work.
  This is the geometry.
  This has always been the geometry.
