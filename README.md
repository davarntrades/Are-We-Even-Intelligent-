## Before We Begin

Most frameworks for intelligence are designed to confirm that the people who built them are intelligent.

IQ tests. Academic credentials. Publication counts. Citation indices. Benchmark scores.

This one is not that.

The Intelligence Invariant™ is a geometric law. It doesn’t care who you are. It doesn’t care what you’ve achieved. It doesn’t care what institution vouches for you.

It asks one question only:

> **Is your reachable future expanding or contracting?**

Everything else follows from the answer.

The difference is not semantic. It is structural. One is a **stock**. The other is a **flow**. And critically — the flow can be **negative**.
